About

I am an experienced IT Technician and frontend developer who is highly motivated, creative, and adaptive. I possess a strong sense of responsibility and am a goal-oriented team player with excellent communication skills. My technical skills include IT support, web development, JavaScript programming, CMS website management, and analytical skills. In terms of education, I hold ITIL V3, WISS Business Informatics Specialist, and multiple Microsoft certifications, including MCITP. I am also skilled in various operating systems, programs, and programming languages. Additionally, I am fluent in German and English and hold a Class B Swiss driver's license. In terms of work experience, I have worked at UBS AG, Hoffmann La Roche AG via Swisslinx, Backerhicks AG, CSF Computer Solution Factory, and Johnson & Johnson IT-Support via ATOS/Brunel, among others. I have provided IT support and developed frontend solutions for various companies, including VIP support for UBS-Traders, local 2nd/3rd level IT support, and system upgrades/migrations. Overall, my experience and technical expertise in IT support and frontend development make me a strong candidate for roles that require these skills.
